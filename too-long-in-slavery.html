Too Long In Slavery

2011-01-14
By Tshepang Lekhonkhobe

The mood of Too Long In Slavery (performed by Culture and found in Greatest Hits - The Frontlne Years) matches it's title pretty well (the vocalist sounds like he's in pain and so is the melody). The track still sounds is near-playful, though it has nearly brought me to tears on a few occasions; it's a powerful delivery of a painful message. It's my favorite among the group's work.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
