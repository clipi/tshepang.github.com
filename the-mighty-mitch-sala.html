the mighty Mitch Sala

2009-02-13
By Tshepang Lekhonkhobe

If ever you admire someone excessively to a point of nearly letting out a tear, you tend to get worried, perhaps due to pride, that you may be too obsessed. This was a case when Mitch Sala, an impossibly-successful Australian businessman, who undertook a job to inspire more people to be successful in Amway. He came through to South Africa and made a powerful presentation, "and I even got to shake his hand!"
