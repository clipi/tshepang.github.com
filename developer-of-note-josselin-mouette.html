developer of note: Josselin Mouette

2011-04-08 by Tshepang Lekhonkhobe

The efforts that Josselin Mouette puts into maintaining Debian's GNOME are amazing (and seemingly thankless). He is the most visible member of that team-maintained part of Debian. He also portrays deep knowledge of GNOME's oft-inconsistent and oft-complex internals, and does participate in upstream discussions.
