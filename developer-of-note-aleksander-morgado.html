developer of note: Aleksander Morgado

2010-05-20
By Tshepang Lekhonkhobe

The Tracker team got this new guy, Aleksander Morgado, doing fabulous work on Tracker's extraction code. He does so while also providing thorough info on his activities.

One thing that prompted me to honor him is his work on adding options to disable stop words, as well as to enable number indexing, fixing an old gripe of mine.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
