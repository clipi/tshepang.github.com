Dry And Heavy (1977)

2012-05-09
By Tshepang Lekhonkhobe

A very laid-back Burning Spear album, with a mere 9 short tracks, the best of which is a re-imagining of one of my eternal favorite tracks of all time, Creation (live versions). It's named It's A Long Way Around, and I could not stop myself from repeating it.

Each of the other tracks is quite good, though not catchy at all.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
