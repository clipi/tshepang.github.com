I recently got hold of "Hold It Down" (1995) by Das EFX. Real good ole-style hip-hop, thanks to a friend who, when I asked who he reckoned the best hip-hop group is, he mentioned Das EFX. In my investigations, I also got hold of their "Generation EFX" (1998) which is really bad. Strange that just 3 years later people can fuck up like that.
