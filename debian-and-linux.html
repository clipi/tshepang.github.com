Debian and Linux

There is a Debian history discussion among Debian old-timers where a point was raised that Ian Murdock, Debian founder, successfully transferred project leadership to someone else, a fact that Bdale Garbee stated to not yet have taken place with Linux, the poster child of FLOSS. It's also interesting to see Murdock praise successive leadership as superior to his.
