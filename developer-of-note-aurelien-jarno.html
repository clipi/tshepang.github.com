developer of note: Aurelien Jarno

In celebration of the beginning of this exercise, I recognize Aurelien Jarno as a very notable developer. This is due to his tireless efforts as the number one GLibC co-maintainer in Debian since several months (if the package's debian changelog is to be relied upon). Also, GLibC, together with GCC and Linux, ought to be regarded as the most important packages of any GNU/Linux system.
