Tshepang logs : Six Shooter (2005)

2013-02-24

This 27 minute short is worthy mainly due to the loud and fascinating young guy sitting next to the lead character on a train trip. He happens to be the most insensitive character I have seen anywhere (if my poor memory serves me well), and is funny at times. The ending is strange though, and I guess deliberately pointless.

I was inspired to watch this due the director, who later on made the weird and wonderful Seven Psychopaths.
