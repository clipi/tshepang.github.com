Floetic (2002)

I love the smooth melody that is *Floetic* by Floetry. It is the sort of sound you find in Jill Scott's near-masterpiece of an album, Who's Jill Scott.

Here's my favorites:

  1. track 5: "Getting Late" – not nearly as seductive as "Say Yes", but what it's a masterwork
  2. track 8: "Say Yes" – it's most notable thing is the sensual play by one them gals; makes me wanna say... yes
  3. track 2: "Floetic" – energetic and nearly-formulaic; beautiful arrangements
