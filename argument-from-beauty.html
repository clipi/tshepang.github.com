Argument from Beauty

Posted on 2010-08-30
by Tshepang Lekhonkhobe
in misc.

So there's a bunch of arguments for the existence of God, and learning about them is actually fun for me. I find the Argument from beauty, though not the strongest of all, the most seductive. It's ever so nice when I joke about it, like when I see a super-hot gal (the sort you'd be glad to eat her shit just to have a taste of her pussy) or enjoying a wonderful meal (like the one I enjoyed last weekend when visiting Botswana). I'd throw something like, "How could such a gal have evolved to this form through blind forces of nature! God-did-it!".
